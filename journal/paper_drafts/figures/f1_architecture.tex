\caption{\textbf{PSM architecture.}
Stage~0: streaming egocentric frames at 1\,fps plus the wearer's
SLAM trajectory.
Stage~1 (\emph{ingest}): each frame is encoded by a swappable
vision--language encoder (CLIP-L / CLIP-bigG / SigLIP\,2) and
deposited into the H3 hex cell containing its $(\text{lat},
\text{lng})$. Per-cell state is \emph{bounded}: a ring buffer of $C$
HyperLogLog sketches (orange; older sketches faded) gives a
time-decayed unique-event cardinality and the
$(t_{\min}, t_{\max})$ envelope; a reservoir of $R$
sampled embeddings (blue) is the bounded-memory substitute for the
linearly-growing CLIP bank.
Stage~2 (\emph{retrieval}): the query text is encoded with the same
encoder and cosine-similarity-searched against the reservoir union;
\caponek{} bounds how many of the $K{=}5$ slots a single cell may
fill, returning candidate $(t, \text{cell}, \text{lat}, \text{lng},
\mathbf{e})$ tuples in sub-millisecond C-side wall time.
Stage~3 (\emph{rerank}): the $K{=}5$ frames are fetched from the
device frame buffer and shipped to a frontier MLLM (Gemini\,3.1\,Pro),
which picks the single best frame index --- the localized answer.}
